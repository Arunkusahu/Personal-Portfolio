Purpose

A standout feature is the reservation system, designed for simplicity and efficiency. Users can select their preferred date and time, choose the number of guests, and even specify seating preferences. The seamless integration with our backend ensures real-time availability, minimizing wait times and enhancing customer satisfaction.The user profile page provides a comprehensive view of past orders, favorite dishes, & saved preferences, allowing for a tailored dining experience.

Accessibility is a priority, with thoughtful considerations such as scalable text, high contrast options, and keyboard navigation support. Mobile responsiveness ensures that our design shines across all devices, from desktops to smartphones.

Problem Solving

"We implemented a straightforward, top-bar menu that categorizes our offerings logically. This intuitive structure allows users to quickly find what they are looking for, reducing frustration and improving overall usability. To ensure visual clarity and accessibility, we opted for a clean layout with high-contrast text, and scalable fonts. Recognizing the common pain point of a complicated reservation process, we designed a user-friendly reservation system.

We addressed the challenge of presenting menu items engagingly by incorporating high-quality images and detailed descriptions. Understanding the importance of mobile responsiveness, we designed the UI to adapt seamlessly across all devices. This ensures that users have a consistent and enjoyable experience, whether they are accessing the site from a desktop, tablet, or smartphone."
